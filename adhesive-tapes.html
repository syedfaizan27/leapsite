Masking Tape

Description & Features

  • Crepe Paper Masking tape
  • Wet Resistant Masking
  • Good Initial Tack
  • Free Residue Removal
  • Easy Masking & De-masking

Application

Masking tape, often called painter's tape, is a versatile, pressure-sensitive adhesive tape made from a thin, easy-to-tear paper backing and an easily released adhesive. Its defining characteristic is its ability to be removed cleanly without leaving residue or damaging the surface, making it ideal for temporary applications.

Double sided cloth tape

Description & Features

  • Hand Tearable

Application

Double-sided cloth tape is a type of pressure-sensitive adhesive tape that features a strong, usually woven, cloth or fabric backing coated with adhesive on both sides The cloth backing provides significant strength, flexibility, and conformability, making it well-suited for a variety of applications where a robust, double-sided bond is needed.
